My Favorite Restaurants

Whenever I travel, I love to check out the local cuisine and the most popular restaurants! SAVANNAH, GA has some of the best eateries and beautiful architecture. Their long standing historic properties nearth Forsyth Park are nicely preserved and even rented out as AirBNBs for tourists. It is the perfect location to venture out to some beautiful sites and get a taste of all the cuisine that Savannah has to offer.

For eclectic Asian gourmet, check out Flying Monk Noodle Bar.

Flying Monk Noodle Bar has such a variety of Asian foods. They cover a range of cuisine from many Asian countries such as:

  • Cambodia, Vietnam, China, Korea, and Japan.

For reimagined Southern eats, check out Treylor Park.

Their menu consists of unique recipes to try out. They are most known for their PB&J chicken wings and their Treylor Park Nachos Grande.

For fine-dining, check out Vic's on the River.

Located on the river, this restaurant is perfect for date night. You can watch the ferry go by and watch the sunset with a drink in your hand.

For dessert, check outLeopold's Ice Cream.

Ice Cream. Do I need to say anything else? PS - they have options for vegans too!

For homemade, check out your kitchen.

Sometimes you gotta save your coins and cook at home. Plus, it's the perfect time to experiment with that recipe you've always wanted to try.
